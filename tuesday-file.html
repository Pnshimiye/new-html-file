First things first : Where and what is Gisenyi

Gisenyi is a city in Rubavu district in Rwanda.
Gisenyi is a town on the north shore of Lake Kivu on the border with DR Congo Gisenyi is coterminous with Goma,
the city across the border in the Democratic Republic of the Congo.
